About Us


MobiWheel, a division of Perfect Mobiles R.G.M Plaza, is an online electronics repair service that dedicates itself towards the highest degree of customer satisfaction. We specialize in fixing smartphones, tablets, laptops, and various other devices that require utmost care and attention.

MobiWheel will free you from the nuisance of dealing with untrustworthy service centers, where you have to deposit your device for an uncertain amount of time. You also risk the theft of personal data and unauthorized tampering of phone components.

With MobiWheel, you won’t have to bother visiting time-consuming, distant repair centers.

In the waiting line at a service center, we’re sure you wished for a Genie that visits your home with workshop in his pocket, and fixes your device with a finger-snap. Mobiwheel is that Genie.

After you book an appointment with MobiWheel citing the device issue(s) in detail, our repair expert will visit you, and fix your device on the spot. Your device will be rendered brand new once we’re done!

Mission

  • To provide a door-stop repairing service that is fair, fast, and reliable
  • To warrant transparent pricing of the repairing services that are only levied after the customer’s problem is completely fixed
  • To provide the best customer experience at every step of our relationship

Vision

  • We strive to make MobiWheel a household name, a brand recognized for rendering quality repair services at affordable prices.
  • We are also determined to keep the environment free from e-waste with the help of green practices and e-recycling. After all, we do not inherit the Earth from our ancestors we borrow it from our children.

The Core Pillars

  • A team of skilled technicians and innovative engineers that guarantees an expert resolution to your problem(s)
  • Renowned for efficiency in the customer service sector
  • A passion to solve problems with innovative approaches using state-of-the-art tools.
  • Assurance of quality at every stage of customer relationship.
